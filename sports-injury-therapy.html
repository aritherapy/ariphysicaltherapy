Whether you're a weekend warrior or a competitive athlete, a sports injury can sideline you fast. At ARI Physical Therapy, we specialize in getting athletes back to peak performance safely — combining sport-specific, one-on-one rehab with injury-prevention strategies to keep you in the game long-term.

What we treat

  • ACL, MCL & rotator cuff injuries
  • Sprains, strains & tendinopathies
  • Knee, shoulder & ankle injuries
  • Overuse & repetitive-strain injuries
  • Return-to-sport programs
  • Performance & injury prevention

Sports injury therapy FAQs

Do I need a referral to start sports injury therapy?
No. In California you can see a physical therapist directly — for up to 12 visits or 45 days, whichever comes first — so you can start without a doctor's referral. Some insurance plans still require a referral for coverage, so we recommend checking with your plan, and we're happy to help.
How soon can I get back to my sport?
It depends on your injury, sport, and goals. After your evaluation, your therapist will map out a realistic timeline and use return-to-sport testing to confirm you're ready before you go back — so you return strong and reduce the risk of re-injury.
Do you treat my sport or injury?
We work with weekend warriors and competitive athletes across all sports, treating everything from acute injuries to overuse problems. Call us at (661) 282-8584 and we'll let you know how we can help.
What happens at the first visit?
Your initial evaluation takes about 45 to 60 minutes. We discuss your history and goals, assess your movement, and begin your personalized rehab plan.
