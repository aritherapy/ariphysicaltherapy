Surgery is only the first step — what happens during recovery determines your outcome. At ARI Physical Therapy, post-operative rehab is one-on-one and coordinated closely with your surgeon's protocol — minimizing scar tissue, restoring mobility, and rebuilding strength in a safe, structured progression.

Surgeries we help you recover from

  • Total joint replacement (knee, hip, shoulder)
  • Spinal surgery rehabilitation
  • ACL & ligament reconstruction recovery
  • Rotator cuff & shoulder surgery rehab
  • Surgical scar & wound management
  • Coordinated care with your surgical team

Post-op rehab FAQs

Do I need a referral for post-operative physical therapy?
In California you can see a physical therapist directly — for up to 12 visits or 45 days — so you can start without a separate referral. Most post-op patients also come in with a surgeon's protocol, and we coordinate directly with your surgical team. Some insurance plans still require a referral for coverage, so we recommend checking with your plan.
When should I start rehab after surgery?
It depends on your procedure and your surgeon's protocol. Many patients begin within days to a few weeks after surgery. We coordinate directly with your surgical team so your rehab starts at the right time and follows the right precautions.
Do you work with my surgeon?
Yes. We build your program around your surgeon's post-operative protocol and coordinate care so everyone is aligned on your recovery timeline and precautions.
What happens at the first visit?
Your initial evaluation takes about 45 to 60 minutes. We review your procedure and your surgeon's protocol, assess your current mobility and strength, and begin your personalized recovery plan.
