Before Your First Visit

What to Bring

Having the right items ready ensures we can get started on your care right away — no delays, no surprises.

  • Photo ID (driver's license or state ID)
  • Insurance card(s) — primary and secondary if applicable
  • Physician referral or prescription, if required by your insurance (if not already sent directly by your doctor)
  • List of current medications
  • Any relevant imaging reports (X-rays, MRI, CT scans)
  • Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing
  • Co-pay (if applicable)

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first appointment.

In California, you can be seen directly by a physical therapist without a physician referral for up to 12 visits or 45 days — whichever comes first. However, some insurance plans still require a referral for coverage, so we recommend calling your insurance beforehand.
Your initial evaluation typically takes 45 to 60 minutes. This includes a thorough assessment, discussion of your goals, and the start of your personalized treatment plan. Follow-up visits are usually 30 to 45 minutes.
Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy access to the area being treated. For example, if you're being treated for a knee injury, shorts are ideal. Athletic wear generally works well.
Some discomfort during therapy is normal, especially early on. Our goal is to work within your tolerance and never push you beyond what's appropriate. Communication with your therapist is key — let them know how you're feeling throughout each session.
Every patient is different. After your initial evaluation, your therapist will outline an estimated treatment plan. Many conditions improve significantly in 6–12 visits, while others may require more time. We'll keep you informed every step of the way.
Yes — free parking is available directly at our Stockdale Hwy location. We are conveniently located at 8200 Stockdale Hwy, Suite B2, Bakersfield, CA 93311.
