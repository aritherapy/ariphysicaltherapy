SMS Terms of Service

Effective date: June 10, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 10, 2026

These SMS Terms of Service ("SMS Terms") govern the text-messaging program operated by ARI Physical Therapy ("ARI Physical Therapy," "we," "us," or "our"). By providing your mobile number and consenting to receive text messages from us, you agree to these SMS Terms. They should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. Program Description

ARI Physical Therapy's text-messaging program sends transactional, service-related messages only to patients who have agreed to receive them. We use text messaging to support your care, including:

  • appointment reminders;
  • appointment confirmations, scheduling, and rescheduling;
  • patient-care notifications related to your visits.

We do not send marketing, advertising, or promotional text messages through this program.

2. How You Sign Up (Consent)

You are enrolled only after you provide consent. Consent is obtained verbally — in person at our clinic or by phone — when you provide your mobile number and agree to receive text messages from us. Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of receiving care or any service from us. You may decline at sign-up or withdraw your consent at any time (see Section 6).

3. Types of Messages You May Receive

Messages are limited to the categories described in Section 1 — appointment reminders, confirmations and scheduling, and patient-care notifications. Messages may come from a phone number or short code associated with our practice or our messaging provider.

4. Message Frequency & Rates

  • Message frequency varies based on your appointments and the needs of your care.
  • Message and data rates may apply. These charges are set by your mobile carrier and plan and are your responsibility. Contact your carrier for details about your plan.

5. Opt Out & Help

STOP

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe at any time. You may also opt out by calling us at (661) 282-8584. After you opt out, we may send one final message confirming you've been unsubscribed, and we will stop sending texts.

HELP

Reply HELP for assistance, or contact us directly at (661) 282-8584 or contactus@ariptcare.com.

If you opt out of text messages, we will continue to contact you about your appointments and care by other means, such as phone or email.

6. Privacy & Your Information

We never share or sell your mobile number or SMS consent. Your mobile information and your opt-in are not shared with or sold to any third parties or affiliates for their marketing purposes. Mobile information is shared only with the messaging providers that operate this texting service on our behalf, solely to deliver the messages you've asked to receive.

Our handling of your information is described in full in our Privacy Policy.

7. Text Messaging Is Not Secure

Standard SMS text messages are not encrypted and are not a fully secure channel. Messages could be seen by anyone with access to your device. To protect your privacy, we keep our messages general and avoid including detailed clinical or sensitive health information. Please do not include sensitive personal or medical details in your replies. If you'd prefer we not contact you by text, let us know and we'll use another method.

8. Carrier Disclaimer

Carriers (including wireless carriers) are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Delivery of messages is subject to effective transmission by your mobile carrier and is not guaranteed. Message delivery is on an "as available" basis.

9. Changes to These Terms

We may update these SMS Terms from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and post the updated terms here. Your continued participation in the text-messaging program after an update means you accept the revised terms.
