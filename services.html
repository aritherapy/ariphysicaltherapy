What We Treat

Our Specialties

Eight specialized programs built around your unique needs — because recovery isn't one-size-fits-all.

At ARI Physical Therapy, every treatment plan starts with listening to you. Whether you're recovering from surgery, managing chronic pain, or looking to stay active and injury-free, our team of certified professionals brings the expertise and compassion to get you there.

01
Orthopedic

Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Musculoskeletal injuries — from fractures and ligament tears to arthritis and joint pain — require precise, targeted therapy to heal correctly and fully. Our orthopedic rehab program combines hands-on manual therapy with progressive exercise to restore strength, range of motion, and function.

  • Joint pain and stiffness
  • Fracture and ligament recovery
  • Hip, knee, shoulder, and spine conditions
  • Arthritis management
Book a Consultation
02
Sports

Sports Injury Recovery

Whether you're a weekend warrior or a competitive athlete, a sports injury can sideline you fast. We specialize in getting athletes back to peak performance safely, combining sport-specific rehab with injury prevention strategies to keep you in the game long-term.

  • ACL, MCL, and rotator cuff injuries
  • Sprains, strains, and tendinopathies
  • Return-to-sport programs
  • Performance optimization
Book a Consultation
03
Post-Surgery

Post-Operative Care

Surgery is only the first step — what happens during recovery determines your outcome. Our post-operative rehab programs are designed in close coordination with your surgeon's protocol to minimize scar tissue, restore mobility, and rebuild strength in a safe, structured progression.

  • Total joint replacement recovery
  • Spinal surgery rehabilitation
  • Surgical wound and scar management
  • Coordinated care with your surgical team
Book a Consultation
04
Chronic Pain

Chronic Pain Rehab

Living with chronic pain is exhausting — and you deserve more than just "managing" it. Our approach addresses the root causes of persistent pain through movement therapy, manual techniques, and patient education, helping you build long-term strategies for a more comfortable, active life.

  • Low back and neck pain
  • Fibromyalgia and widespread pain
  • Headaches and TMJ dysfunction
  • Pain education and self-management tools
Book a Consultation
05
Pelvic Health

Pelvic Pain

Pelvic floor dysfunction is far more common than most people realize — and far more treatable. Our certified pelvic health therapists provide discreet, evidence-based care for a range of pelvic conditions affecting both men and women, restoring comfort and confidence.

  • Pelvic floor muscle dysfunction
  • Urinary and bowel incontinence
  • Pelvic organ prolapse
  • Pelvic pain and interstitial cystitis
Book a Consultation
06
Pediatric

Pediatric Rehabilitation

Children have unique physical and developmental needs. Our pediatric therapists create a fun, encouraging environment where kids can heal, grow, and reach their milestones. We work closely with families to ensure progress continues at home as well as in the clinic.

  • Developmental delays and coordination
  • Sports injuries in young athletes
  • Post-surgical pediatric rehab
  • Torticollis and musculoskeletal conditions
Book a Consultation
07
Women's Health

Pre / Post Partum Rehab

Pregnancy and childbirth put enormous demands on the body — demands that don't disappear once baby arrives. We support women through every stage, from prenatal body changes to postpartum recovery, with a focus on pelvic health, core strength, and return to activity.

  • Prenatal back and pelvic pain
  • Diastasis recti (abdominal separation)
  • C-section and birth recovery
  • Postpartum return-to-exercise guidance
Book a Consultation
08
Wellness

Health Programs

Great health doesn't stop when your formal therapy ends. Our wellness programs are designed to keep you moving, strong, and injury-free for the long term. Whether you're focused on fall prevention, general fitness, or maintaining the gains from therapy, we have a program for you.

  • Fall prevention for older adults
  • Injury prevention and movement screening
  • Strength and conditioning programs
  • Post-discharge wellness maintenance
Book a Consultation
