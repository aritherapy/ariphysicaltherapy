Frequently Asked Questions

Physical therapy in Bakersfield, CA — referrals, insurance, what to expect, and more.

Answers to the questions we hear most from new and prospective patients. Don’t see yours? Get in touch — we’re happy to help.

Do I need a doctor's referral to start physical therapy?

In California, you can be seen directly by a physical therapist without a physician referral for up to 12 visits or 45 days — whichever comes first. However, some insurance plans still require a referral for coverage, so we recommend calling your insurance beforehand.

What conditions and services do you treat?

We provide one-on-one care across eight specialties: orthopedic rehabilitation, sports injury recovery, post-operative care, chronic pain rehabilitation, pelvic pain therapy, pediatric rehabilitation, pre- and post-partum rehab, and health & wellness programs. See our Services page for details.

Do you offer pelvic floor and women's health therapy?

Yes. Pelvic pain therapy is one of our specialties, provided one-on-one with a licensed physical therapist. Call us at (661) 282-8584 to learn whether it's right for you.

How long will my first appointment take?

Your initial evaluation typically takes 45 to 60 minutes. This includes a thorough assessment, discussion of your goals, and the start of your personalized treatment plan. Follow-up visits are usually 30 to 45 minutes.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Please bring a photo ID (driver's license or state ID), your insurance card(s) — primary and secondary if applicable — and a physician referral or prescription if your insurance requires one (if it hasn't already been sent directly by your doctor).

What should I wear to my appointment?

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy access to the area being treated. For example, if you're being treated for a knee injury, shorts are ideal. Athletic wear generally works well.

Will physical therapy hurt?

Some discomfort during therapy is normal, especially early on. Our goal is to work within your tolerance and never push you beyond what's appropriate. Communication with your therapist is key — let them know how you're feeling throughout each session.

How many visits will I need?

Every patient is different. After your initial evaluation, your therapist will outline an estimated treatment plan. Many conditions improve significantly in 6–12 visits, while others may require more time. We'll keep you informed every step of the way.

Do you accept my insurance?

ARI Physical Therapy accepts most private insurance plans and Medicare. Because coverage and referral requirements vary by plan, call us at (661) 282-8584 and we'll verify your benefits before your first visit.

Do you offer telehealth or virtual visits?

Our physical therapy is hands-on and provided in person at our Bakersfield clinic, 8200 Stockdale Hwy, Suite B2. We don't currently offer virtual visits.

How do I book an appointment?

You can request your first visit online through our appointment page, or call us at (661) 282-8584. New patients can also complete the intake paperwork online ahead of time to save time at check-in.

What is your cancellation policy?

If you need to cancel or reschedule, we ask for at least 24 hours' notice so we can offer your appointment time to another patient who needs care.

What languages do you speak?

Our team speaks English, Spanish, and Hindi.

Where are you located, and is parking available?

Yes — free parking is available right at our location, and we're easy to find: we're at 8200 Stockdale Hwy, Suite B2, Bakersfield, CA 93311, in the same shopping center as Trader Joe's — right next door.

What are your hours?

We're open Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Still have questions?

Call us or request a consultation and we’ll point you in the right direction.
